Quick AI Hunting Tips

  • Focus on data quality ‚Äî garbage in, garbage out. Clean and label your datasets for better model accuracy.
  • Avoid over-reliance on automation; human hunters must validate AI findings to reduce false positives.
  • Keep your models updated with the latest threat intelligence to prevent them from becoming obsolete.
  • Monitor for "model drift" where the accuracy of your detection decays as attacker behaviors evolve.
